# Custom layout setup

1. Press **Win+Shift+'** to open the zones editor.
2. Click the **Create new layout** button at the bottom-right.
3. Choose your preferred zone layout - **Grid** or **Canvas**: 
   
   - [**Grid** model](grid%20model)

   - [**Canvas** model](canvas%20model)
    
4. Click **Save & apply**.
